World Class Library

V.V.P. Library Knowledge Centre to enhance the relevant knowledge for students and faculties available in 12500 square meter area. The Library is equipped with user-friendly web-based ERP Software (In-house developed Library Management Software) that facilitates all library activities. Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) is also available on the Internet for inquiring the status of the resources. Bar-coding system is in use to computerize the bibliographic details of the resources. Currently 55816 library books of worth Rs. 1,95,16,478/-are available. Various journals and magazines are subscribed for the enhancement of knowledge. In addition to that Library has large collection of value based general books. Special reference section is available for the books like competitive exams, data books, guide, encyclopedias, and other useful technical books. Audio Visual room is available for presentation. Library has purchased NPTEL video lectures of 1.5 TB with dedicated server for students access. Online e-journals and e-books are being accessed through NDLI membership (National Digital Library of India) and DELNET. Unique facility of Book Bank is also available for all the semester for all the students on demand bases. The Information data bank is constantly updated and the facilities are added periodically to keep in pace with the recent ICT developments in the various area of knowledge.

Largest Playground

Sports is an integral part of the student's life and in general. Sports relaxes mind and body. It gives immense pleasure. Sports is a recreational activity that enhances working efficiency of internal and external organs of a human body. It is a tool to enjoy stress free life. V.V.P. Engineering College has a well-established sports culture. The department of sports @ V.V.P. is organizing the institute level tournaments twice a year that is once in a semester. The institute has a fully furnished grounds of Cricket with a turf wicket, Basketball, Volleyball, Kabaddi and Kho-Kho along with all indoor game facilities like chess, carom, badminton and table tennis. The students are participating in Chess, Badminton, Carom and Table Tennis in monsoon semester named as “Spirit Indoor” and they are enjoying a sports festival “Spirit Outdoor” in the month of January and February with the games like Cricket, Volleyball, Basketball and Kho-Kho. The students are also participating in the GTU zonal and Inter-Zonal games and in the last three years only VVP has won more than 10 titles and more than 50 students have become a national player.

About us

Vyavasayi Vidya Pratishthan (V.V.P.) Trust was set up in 1996. “राष्ट्राय स्वाहा इदं न मम” is the moto of this public charitable trust with the aim to contribute in the field of education on non-profit base. The Trust prides itself on establishment of the first engineering college in Rajkot, the central location of Saurashtra and Kutch region 25 years back. The philanthropic inclination of the trust has been a driving force for the college to succeed over the years.
